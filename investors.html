Why we created
Pax Wallet
Blockchain and cryptocurrencies introduced the world to a new financial system — DeFi. However, these solutions are not perfect: they are complex and scattered in different networks. We have combined the best solutions in one app to make DeFi more accessible.
What we are dealing with
1
Interface complexity
  • Beginners unfriendly
  • Overloaded with functions
  • Requires to interact with multiple additional apps
“One click” interactions
Intuitive interface. The user does not need to dive into the processes of the application function. It is enough to select any asset, press the button, and start earning.
2
Manual asset
management
  • Unstable interest rates
  • Time costly
  • Too many protocols with different rates each
Automated
agregated staking
Real-time smart contract algorithm collects many different profitability protocols and automatically determines the most profitable ones
3
Unfairness and issues
with governance
  • Community does not inlvoved in crucial decision making process
  • Governance tokens are concentrated among small and privileged members group
  • Therefore, other holders do not have incentive to hold governance token
Decentralized
autonomous organization
Through the distribution of the PWT token, holders will be able to participate in making key decisions on the development of the platform and put forward their proposals for a general vote.
